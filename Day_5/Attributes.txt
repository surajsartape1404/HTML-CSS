Attributes: 
    1. Providing additional information about an element. 
    2. we can make html element address by the help of attributes.

common attributes examples:  rel, href, type, src, alt, height, width, class, id
    title, target, controls,  step, start,  selected, required,  readonly, placeholder, disabled, style.
